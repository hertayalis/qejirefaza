Charlie Ebersol is a TV producer born on December 30, 1982 in Torrington, CT. He co-founded a production company called The Company with Justin Hochberg in 2011. The Company has produced a variety of television shows, including the Emmy-nominated documentary series The Moment and the reality competition series The Partner.
