Mira Quin Baila 2021 Premieres In March: What Stars Will Dance?

 Posted on June 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  595 words  |  Martina Birk

The Univision network announced the eight stars that will participate in the new season of the successful competition “Mira Quien Baila” that will premiere on Sunday, March 14 at 8:00 PM ET.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Mira Quién Baila (@miraquienbaila)

Chiquinquirá Delgado and Borja Voces will be the official hosts of “Mira Quien Baila 2021”, a very special season of the competition for having Univision talents exclusively as participants.

The ninth season of “Mira Quien Baila” is the first edition of the important dance competition that will not have Javier Poza as the official host of the show.

The jury for this new edition will be made up of the actress and singer Patricia Manterola, the creative director and professional dancer Casper Smart, and the former Miss Universe and winner of the fifth season of “Mira Quien Baila”, Dayanara Torres.

The Puerto Rican star Kiara Liz, winner of the latest edition of “Mira Quien Baila”, will debut as the host of everything that happens backstage at the competition. In addition, the talented choreographer and professional dancer Toni Costa returns to Univision as a mentor to the participants of the new season of the television production.

“Mira Quien Baila” has aired on Univision for eight seasons, the last edition was held in 2020. At that time, Kiara Liz managed to win a prize of $ 25,000 for her charity. preference.
