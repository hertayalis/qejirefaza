WWE Royal Rumble Pre-Sale Ticket Demand Cripples MLB Box Office Website

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  379 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

WWE brings a ton of attention for their big events, and the 2024 Royal Rumble is set to be banner exhibition for the company. They are also getting things off to a very strong start, as even demand for pre-sale tickets has crippled the Major League Baseball box office.

The 37th Royal Rumble event will take place at Tropicana Field on Saturday, January 27th. This will also be the first Royal Rumble event under TKO’s leadership after WWE and UFC’s merger. There is a lot of hype around the event, and that is showing in a big way.

Pre-sale tickets for the 2024 WWE Royal Rumble event went up today on the MLB website. Those tickets might not last long, but fans are also having a ton of trouble getting tickets for the big show.

“We’re currently experiencing some technical difficulties, and are working to fix them as soon as possible. Please check back and try again later.

If this problem persists, please contact the Ticket Box Office.“

The seating chart revealed exactly how WWE is setting up the show. Luckily for those Superstars, it doesn’t appear that they will have a long trek to the ring for the Royal Rumble match. Tropicana field will still be packed full of fans, even if they have to go through a lot of trouble to get those tickets.

As a former host of the ThunderDome during the pandemic, that building was home to several WWE events. This time around, they won’t have virtual fans, because the stadium will be packed with actual human beings.

We will monitor the 2024 Royal Rumble event as we near the conclusion of 2023. It promises to be an important year, and TKO may aim to kickstart their journey towards their first WrestleMania with a strong start.
