Karrion Kross Appears to Set His Sights on Roman Reigns in Unseen WWE SmackDown Footage

 Posted on June 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  235 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Karrion Kross had a memorable run in NXT, where he won the NXT Championship twice. However, all of that went to waste after his initial main roster run ended up destroying his reputation and led to his release. Kross has been absent from WWE television for the past few months but now it appears he seemingly targeted Roman Reigns during Smackdown this week.

Kross returned to WWE and immediately attacked Drew McIntyre during the closing moments of SmackDown back in August last year. He had a long feud with McIntyre and then began a feud with Rey Mysterio and now with AJ Styles.

While The Harbinger Of Doom presents himself as a legitimate threat to his opponents, his booking has only shown the opposite. That said, he took to his Instagram and uploaded a video, where he could be seen targeting Roman Reigns as the latter made his entrance during Smackdown this week – making it clear he wants The Tribal Chief.

Ringside News exclusively reported that WWE plans to make good use of Karrion Kross with new push. It remains to be seen when he will return to WWE television, as a feud with Roman Reigns would certainly inject life into his career.
